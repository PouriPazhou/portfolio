Workflow Automation – Cooks for Hire

Problem

Client intake data was collected via paper forms, email, and messaging platforms, requiring duplicate manual entry into accounting and scheduling systems.

Approach

  • Conducted As-Is process mapping
  • Gathered business requirements & user stories
  • Evaluated 3 solution options
  • Designed Zoho Forms + Zapier integration

Tools Used

Zoho Forms, Zapier, Excel, Lucidchart

Results

  • Reduced manual data entry by 60% within 10 weeks
  • Reduced reporting effort by 30%
  • Secured executive sign-off and adoption
